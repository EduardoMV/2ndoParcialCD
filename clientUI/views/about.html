How to play

Welcome to Blackjack! The goal of the game is to beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

To start, click the "Start Playing" button above. You will be dealt two cards, and the dealer will also receive two cards, one face up and one face down.

You can choose to "hit" (receive another card) or "stand" (keep your current hand). Be careful not to exceed 21, as that results in a bust and you lose the round.

After you stand, the dealer will reveal their facedown card and draw cards until their hand totals 17 or higher.

Whoever has the highest hand value without exceeding 21 wins the round!
